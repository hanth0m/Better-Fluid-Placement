# Better Fluid Placement

## This mod gives you more control over how your fluids are placed with a bucket

### Waterlogging
- When sneaking, water is placed next to a waterlogable block instead of waterlogging it no matter what

![Waterloggng behaviour](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/0c1e114501aad3e86567ee1271f39beee5d8b2e0.jpeg)

### Placement on small plants
- When placing fluids on a samll plant-like block (short grass, small flowers, fern, pink petals, saplings, etc.) you can control the placement also by sneaking
  
![Small Plant behaviour](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/a6fdc95e7d6b0aee868b20d66249b519b81c0bee.jpeg)
- If you sneak while placing, the fluid will be placed above the plant (like in vanilla)
- If you do not sneak while placing, the fluid replaces the plant block immediately like in older minecraft versions
